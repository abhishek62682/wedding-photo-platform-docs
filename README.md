# Wedding Photo Finder Platform

## Project Proposal & Feature Documentation

---

# 1. Project Overview

The **Wedding Photo Finder Platform** is an AI-powered web application designed to simplify wedding photo delivery.

Instead of manually sending photos to every guest, the photographer uploads all event photos to Google Drive, creates an event, and shares a unique QR Code.

Guests simply scan the QR Code, enter their details, take a selfie, and instantly receive all the photos in which they appear.

The platform provides a modern, professional, and hassle-free experience for photographers, wedding clients, and guests.

---

# 2. How the Platform Works

### Step 1

Photographer uploads all wedding photos to Google Drive.

↓

### Step 2

Create a new event inside the dashboard.

↓

### Step 3

Connect the Google Drive folder with the event.

↓

### Step 4

The system automatically processes all photos.

↓

### Step 5

A unique Event URL and QR Code are generated.

↓

### Step 6

Photographer shares the QR Code with guests.

↓

### Step 7

Guests scan the QR Code.

↓

### Step 8

Guests enter:

* Name
* Mobile Number
* Email (Optional)

↓

### Step 9

Guest takes a selfie.

↓

### Step 10

AI searches all event photos.

↓

### Step 11

Guest instantly views and downloads their matching photos.
